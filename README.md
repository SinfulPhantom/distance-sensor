# Wiring
## Ultrasonic Sensor
- Connect Vcc to power - 5V
- Connect Trig to DIGITAL 13
- Connect Echo to DIGITAL 10
- Connect Gnd to GND
## 8x8 Matrix
- Connect CLK to ANALOG 5
- Connect DAT to ANALOG 4
- Connect GND to GND
- Connect VCC+ to power - 5V, but 3V works as well
